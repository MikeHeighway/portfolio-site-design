About Work

They're Making Me Do This.

Mike Heighway was born in Ontario, Canada and lived most of his life in New Mexico. He studied architecture at The University of New Mexico, graduating in 2010 with a Master of Architecture. After graduating, he went on to do work for Apple Computer, Hewlett Packard, and several internationally published architects. Prior to forming Obsidian with Mark Jackson-Weaver, he worked as a print and motion designer for a national television network.

Mike is a diverse multidisciplinary designer. His work spans across architectural design, identity, branding, 3D visualization, motion graphics, websites, user interface, app design and other media. In addition he is engaged in the design community, having been a visiting critic at UNM’s School of Architecture and Planning.
